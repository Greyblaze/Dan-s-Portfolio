Tic-Tac-Toe

Description: A recreation of the popular 'Tic-Tac-Toe' game. A game-board was created and styled using CSS. Numbers (0-8) were assigned to represent cells on the game-board as combinations. The logic behind this was to create a function which will fill each cell on the game-board, alternating between both players(X & O). Functions were also written to 'check for wins' and 'check for draws' depending on the combination, with alerts popping up at the top of the screen to declare the outcome and to restart the game. Knowledge of if-else statements was crucial in building this project.
